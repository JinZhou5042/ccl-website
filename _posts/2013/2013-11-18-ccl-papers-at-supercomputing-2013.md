     Automated Packaging of Bioinformatics Workflows for Portability and Durability Using Makeflow
    </a>
    at the Workshop on Scientific Workflows (WORKS).
   </li>

    Patrick Donnelly presented
    <a href="http://www.nd.edu/~ccl/research/papers/confuga-discs-2013.pdf">
