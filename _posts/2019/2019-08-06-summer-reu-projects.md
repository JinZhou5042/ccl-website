<a href="http://parsl-project.org/">Parsl workflow language</a>

and the

<a href="http://ccl.cse.nd.edu/software/workqueue/">Work Queue execution framework</a>

, demonstrating Python applications running on thousands of cores, and also streamlined our software installation process via Pip and Conda.  They presented their work a the summer REU poster symposium in Jordan Hall:
