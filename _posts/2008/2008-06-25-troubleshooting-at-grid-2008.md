  David Cieslak will present a paper titled
  <a href="http://www.cse.nd.edu/~ccl/research/pubs/debug-grid08.pdf">
   Troubleshooting Thousands of Jobs on Production Computing Grids Using Data Mining Techniques

  at
  <a href="http://www.grid2008.org">
   Grid 2008
  </a>
  in Japan.  This work demonstrates techniques for drawing conclusions such as "Your jobs fail on Linux 2.4 with less than 16GB RAM" from complex workloads of thousands of jobs.
