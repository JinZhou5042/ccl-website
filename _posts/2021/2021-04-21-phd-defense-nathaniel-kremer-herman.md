<p>
   Congratulations to Dr. Kremer-Herman, who successfully defended his Ph.D. dissertation
   <a href="http://ccl.cse.nd.edu/research/papers/nkremerh-dissertation.pdf">
    "Log Discovery, Log Custody, and the Web Inspired Approach for Open Distributed Systems Troubleshooting"
   </a>
   .  His work created a system TLQ (Troubleshooting via Log Queries) that enables the structured query of distributed data logged by independent components including workflows, batch systems, and application file access.  Prof. Kremer-Herman recently began a faculty position at
   <a href="https://www.hanover.edu/academics/programs/cs">
    Hanover College
   </a>
   in Indiana.  Congrads!
  </p>
