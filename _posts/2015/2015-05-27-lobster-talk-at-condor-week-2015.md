Ben Tovar gave an overview of Lobster in the talk

<a href="http://ccl.cse.nd.edu/research/presentations/lobster-condor-week-2015/lobster-condor-week-2015.pdf">High-Energy Physics workloads on 10k non-dedicated opportunistic cores with Lobster</a>

. The talk was part of Condor Week 2015, at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

<br/>

<br/>

Lobster is a system for deploying data intensive high-throughput science applications on non-dedicated resources. It is build on top Work Queue, Parrot, and Chirp, which are part of CCTools.
